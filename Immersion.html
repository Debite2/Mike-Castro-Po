Castro, Mike Lyan V. Castro

Week 1 Week 2

Week 1

The student’s immersion at PCSO Calapan from January 13–17, 2025, involved a mix of administrative and ICT tasks. On the first day, they assisted with surveys and office tasks like passing documents. They learned about PCSO's lottery system on day two and helped with document scanning. On day three, they participated as judges for the small-town lottery. Days four and five involved continued administrative work and the compilation of a "Masterlist of Official Documents for 2023-2024," where the student applied their ICT skills. The experience provided exposure to various office operations and responsibilities.

Week 2

From January 20–22, 2025, the student’s immersion at PCSO Calapan involved various administrative and office tasks. On Day 6, they assisted with medical surveys, compiled and revised files, and helped with lotto tickets. On Day 7, they finalized a file but discovered necessary revisions. On Day 8, they worked on the file revisions and assisted with stamping a document for scanning. In the afternoon, they created a map of agents. On Day 9, they worked on placing agents' names on the map but encountered a mistake with tarpaulin size. On Day 10, they helped with printing and finalizing the map and scanned documents.
